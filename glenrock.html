My Guide to Glen Rock

By Elana Altman

Glen Rock, NJ is a small town in Northern New Jersey. My family moved there when I was four years old, and I lived there until I went to college. I still go back and visit about once a month - so let me show you around!

My Favorite Spots

Restaurants

  • Tani
  • The Kosher Nosh

Attractions

  • The Rock
  • Saddle River County Park
  • The Farmer's Market
  • The Thielke Aroboretum

Getting Around Town

Since Glen Rock is less than 1 square mile in area, you can usually walk around without much difficulty! There are also two train lines and multiple bus lines, which is helpful if you want to go into NYC or a neighboring town.
